Produce Processor

Handoff / maintenance doc · current as of 2026-04-22 · v2.15.0

What it does

Brief: Shared real-time task list on iPads for the produce-processing crew.

General: A receiving manager uploads a dated CSV (from IT's PDF/CSV export) via a desktop browser. The app parses it, writes items to Firebase Realtime Database, and iPads on the floor sync live — staff begin/complete items, record short WebM instructional videos keyed by item ID, and capture completion photos. Stats and timing data accumulate per item. Completed items auto-clean after 10 days. iPad vs desktop UI is chosen by user-agent detection.
